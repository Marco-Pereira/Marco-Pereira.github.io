BoraNow

BoraNow, a travel guide with the city of Lisbon in mind. It´s a web application that helps people find places they like, based on a quiz that delivers personalized suggestions, based on the visitor's likes, it provides many informacion about the places, if it is Covid-19 safe (based ont the mesasures taken by the places), feedback left by other people, save quiz results, and others . It´s also a platform for companies and entities to market their places and find new clients, the platform privides with tools to manage their places. Technologies used included C#, ASP.NET, .NET CORE, SQL, HTML, CSS, BOOTSTRAP, RAZOR and GITHUB. N-Tier architecture and MVC design pattern.
